## Body

Hi,

There is a new free tool on Become a Writer Today: the Writing Dashboard.

It tracks your writing streak, your best single day, your best rolling week, and your full writing history as a year-at-a-glance heatmap. Every number comes from words you log yourself. Nothing is stored on a server. Everything stays in your browser.

[Open the Writing Dashboard](https://becomeawritertoday.com/dashboard)

If you have already used the Book Deadline Planner to log word counts, those words will appear in the dashboard automatically. The two tools share the same local log, so nothing carries over or needs re-entering.

The dashboard also has a word sprint timer. Pick 10, 15, or 25 minutes, write in the sprint area, and the tool records your personal-best words-per-minute. At the end of a sprint, you can add the words directly to today's log.

Writing streaks are one of the most reliable habit-forming mechanisms in existence. A daily record of how many days in a row you showed up turns writing from a mood into a practice. The dashboard gives you that record without asking for an account or an email address.

[Start your writing streak today](https://becomeawritertoday.com/dashboard)

Bryan Collins
